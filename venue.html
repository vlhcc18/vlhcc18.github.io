Venue

VL/HCC 2018 take place in Lisbon at the Rectorate Building of Universidade Nova de Lisboa (UNL). This complex is located in Campolide, on one of the seven hills of Lisbon. The Rectorate building was awarded the Lisbon’s Valmor Prize for Architecture in 2002.

Please check here for more information on the city, places to visit, and other points of interest.

Lisbon

Getting the feel of a city involves a mixture of observation and experience that only come when we allow ourselves to be drawn into its daily life and discover its smells, sounds, voices, lanes and hidden corners.

In Lisbon, the Tagus river is never far away as we explore the history-laden streets of a city that constantly confronts us with its past, as the decorative cobble pavements lead us up from the Praca do Comercio through the 18th century Lower Town to St. George's Castle.

From the Castle, we can enjoy unparalleled views of the south bank, Rossio, the Carmo Convent, the Bairro Alto, the Avenida da Liberdade and Parque Eduardo VII, Nossa Senhora do Monte and the Mount of Graca. It could be for all the world a picture postcard of Lisboa under a perpetually blue sky, reflecting the varied colours of the roofs, streets, old houses, window frames and green spaces, offset by the glittering stream of the Tagus! (adapted from CM-Lisboa)

Travel

From Lisbon airport

Lisbon-airport is only 10 minutes away (7 km) from the conference venue, located at Reitoria of Universidade Nova de Lisboa. See a list of available options.

By car

  • Leave the Airport plaza and follow the signs for (Segunda Circular / E01 / Av. General Norton de Matos) towards the (Eixo Norte-Sul / IP7).
  • Drive 6.6 km along the motorway and follow the direction (Saida 6 towards the Eixo Norte-Sul / A2 / Sul).
  • Drive 2 km along the Eixo Norte-Sul / IP7 and the exit toward Praca de Espanha.
  • Keep right at the fork, follow signs for Praca de Espanha and merge onto Av. dos Combatentes.
  • Slight left to stay on Av. dos Combatentes for about 1.5 km, and take the exit toward Campolide/Amoreiras.
  • Merge onto R. de Campolide and then turn left onto Tv. Estevao Pinto. Continue straight until reach the conference venue, located at Reitoria of Universidade Nova de Lisboa, Campolide Campus.
Directions

By public transportation

Metro

Lisbon airport can be reached directly by subway. The subway station is called Aeroporto and it is the end of the red line as demonstrated in this Metro map. At the airport, you have to follow the metro sign that will lead you to the station. That Metro Station operates from 06:30 a.m. to 01:00 a.m. Take the subway to the last station in the red line, called S. Sebastiao. From there it will take you a 10m walk to reach the conference venue. We suggest this route.

A single Metro ticket costs 1.40€. The travel requires the prior acquisition of Electronic Card "7 Colinas / Viva Viagem", which costs 0.50€. To travel during 24 hours, the ticket is 6€. For more information about fares go here.

Directions


Aerobus

You can also take the Aerobus (2 - Financial Center line), at the Airport Plaza, to Av. Jose Malhoa, a 24 minute travel. It operates from 7:30 a.m. to 11 p.m. everyday. Have a 10-minute walk to conference venue. The 24h Aerobus ticket costs 3.5€.

By taxi

A taxi ride from the Airport to the conference venue costs around 10-15€.


By Uber

The Uber service is available in Lisbon. Costs are around 8-11€ for uberX/uberGREEN, and 20€ for UberBlack (from Airport to Universidade Nova de Lisboa).
